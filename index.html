Reviews

"What a beautiful place to visit and a wonderful house to stay in. There was heaps of room yet still so cosy, with beautifully comfortable beds (all with electric blankets), a lovely fireplace (lots of wood supplied) and an amazingly well-stocked kitchen.
With just 3 minutes walk to gorgeous beaches, shops, cafes, restaurants and even a cinema we had a wonderful time and couldn't recommend Addie McGinty's highly enough!"

Lisa W. October 2018

"We had a lovely Easter stay at Addie's with my in-laws and pre-school children.
The cottage was great for our needs: clean, light and cosy, with lots of clothes storage space and room for all our baby gear. The short walks to the main street, beach and foreshore playground were fine for little legs and prams.
The wood fire was great fun on the one rainy night. Hopefully we can come again for a wintery long weekend and give it a proper workout!"

Francis F. March 2018
